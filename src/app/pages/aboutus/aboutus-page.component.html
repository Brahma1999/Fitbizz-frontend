About Us

Hello ! Fitbizz is my project.

I decided to focus on tracking BMI data and diet plans because i have noticed that people's eating habits in modern days lack specific categories of food with mostly only fat, starch , and sugar for a meal.

Busy schedules also lead to people eating on the run,which results in poor nutritional intake.

I hope Fitbizz will help users achieve a healthier lifestyle by monitoring BMI results and tracking daily food intake.

Feature 1: Calculate BMI
User can calculate BMI by providing weight and height.
Feature 2: Diet Plan & Healthy Recipes
User can view a diet plan and some healthy and tasty recipes.
Feature 3: Make Your Own Diet
User can now add their fav food items and can keep record of nutrients.
